Space Crusade is a small arcade-style shooter in which you play as a small spacecraft equipped with lasers and missiles defending against an onslaught of enemies. It features customizable ship colors, three different types of enemy ships, power-ups, and more! Created in the Summer of 2014.

How to Play: Aim your ship with the mouse and move using WASD. Left click to shoot lasers, and right click to shoot missiles. Navigate menus with the mouse.

Software Requirements: Windows XP, Windows 7, Windows 8 (untested), or Windows 10 (untested)

Hardware Requirements: None
