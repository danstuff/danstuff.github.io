SPOOKY BEAT!

Spooky Beat! is a small rhythm game where you must tap the number keys 1 through 4 to the beat in order to destroy an onslaught of skulls. It features all your three favorite Halloween songs, and several different game modes! Tap the number keys 1 through 4 to destroy skulls. Navigate menus with the mouse. Created in October, 2014.
