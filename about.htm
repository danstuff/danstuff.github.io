DANIEL J. YOST is a programmer, writer, and actor currently enrolled at the Union County Magnet High School of New Jersey. He is highly proficient in Java, Processing, HTML, and C++, having created a wide variety of programs in each. As a writer and actor, Dan has been successful in the Union County Vocational-Technical Schools Drama Club. There, he wrote and directed the comedic performance Club Logistics and acted in a multitude of others, all with great success.
