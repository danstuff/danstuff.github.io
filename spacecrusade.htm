SPACE CRUSADE

Space Crusade is a small arcade-style shooter in which you play as a small spacecraft equipped with lasers and missiles defending against an onslaught of enemies. It features customizable ship colors, three different types of enemy ships, power-ups, and more! Aim your ship with the mouse and move using WASD. Left click to shoot lasers, and right click to shoot missiles. Navigate menus with the mouse. Created in the Summer of 2014.
