HACKMAIL

HackMail is a marketing tool used in preparation for HackUC, a hackathon on the UCVTS campus. HackMail uses the Zoho Mail service to automate the mass-distribution of emails without the recipients seeing who else the email was sent to. Most importantly, HackMail features auto-filling to personalize the title and contents of each email; it replaces "&COMP" and "&NAME" markers with the name of the company and recipient. Created in the Winter of 2016.
