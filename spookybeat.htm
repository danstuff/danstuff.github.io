Spooky Beat! is a small rhythm game where you tap the number keys 1 through 4 to the beat in order to destroy the skulls. It features all your three favorite Halloween songs, and several different game modes! Created in October, 2014.

How to Play: Tap the number keys 1 through 4 to destroy skulls. Navigate menus with the mouse.
